About Suma

I’m Suma Shamanna, an Oregonian living in Bangalore, India. My leadership journey began in Oregon with Congressman David Wu and has grown through grassroots activism, global engagement, and digital innovation. As your Asia-Pacific Regional Vice Chair and Chief Information Security Officer for Democrats Abroad, I lead with compassion, measurable impact, and inclusivity.
