About Suma Shamanna

My name is Suma Shamanna, a proud Oregonian living in Bangalore, India. Throughout my life, I have been driven by a commitment to empower communities through compassion, inclusivity, and purposeful action. My journey began with grassroots activism in Oregon, working with Congressman David Wu’s campaigns. Since then, I have expanded my efforts globally, leading impactful initiatives for Democrats Abroad. Currently, I serve as your Asia-Pacific Regional Vice Chair and as the Chief Information Security Officer for Democrats Abroad.

My leadership is defined by action, measurable outcomes, and a deep sense of compassion and inclusivity. I believe in the power of teamwork, mentorship, and technology to drive real results. With your support, I look forward to continuing this important work and further strengthening our vibrant community.
