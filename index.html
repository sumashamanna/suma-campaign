About Suma

My name is Suma Shamanna, a proud Oregonian living in Bangalore, India. I’ve been driven by a commitment to empower communities through compassion, inclusivity, and purposeful action. From grassroots activism in Oregon to global leadership with Democrats Abroad, I now serve as your Asia-Pacific Regional Vice Chair and Chief Information Security Officer. I believe in teamwork, mentorship, and using technology to deliver real results.

Accomplishments
